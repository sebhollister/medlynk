How it Works

For any PPE Type, our model predicts days on hand using live local data, external trends, and population flow.

A predictive model that's easy to use

You only need to know your current PPE stock, we handle the rest. Have more information such as your historic use rate? Head over to the advanced input tab to get a more specialized model.

Behind the scenes, we crunch the numbers

We take into account live local data, including varying infection rates, case flow between counties, and hospital size and location.

Concise results, including an accurate days on hand projection.

The results dashboard displays not only the projected days on hand for the PPE type, but also the supply curve for each day in the future so you can plan critical orders days in advance.

Prioritize hospitals within a network

Rank hospitals in your network by predicted burn rate, and prioritize PPE supply to the most critical hospitals.
