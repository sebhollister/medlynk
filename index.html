How it Works

Local Data

We predict and use trend data for covid 19 cases on the county level.

Individualized PPE Consumption

We have the ability to work with hospitals to develop the model specifically to their needs.

Population Flow

We model the case flow from surrounding counties to large population centers

Support for Sizing

Currently working on support for different sizes of PPE

Hospital Specific

We use a hospitals unique attributes, like bed size and location, to power an individualized prediction for each hospital

Beyond COVID

Our model can easily be adapted to predict PPE demand based on other driving forces, such as general upper respiratory infections
